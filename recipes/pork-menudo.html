How to Make Pork Menudo

Experience the culinary of the Philippines with our Pork Menudo recipe. This beloved dish features tender pork cubes cooked in a savory tomato-based sauce with garlic, onions, and tomatoes. Over the years, different regions in the Philippines have put their own spin on Pork Menudo, incorporating local ingredients like raisins, hotdogs, or pineapple chunks for a unique flavor profile. Despite the variations, this recipe remains a timeless favorite, cherished for its robust flavors and cultural significance.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 pc Potato
  • 1 pc Carrot
  • 0.5 kg Pork Shoulder
  • 2 sachets Maggi Magic Sarap 8g
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 pc Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 pc Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 pc Bay Leaf
  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 2.5 cups Water
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Sauté potato and carrot in 1 tbsp oil until brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. Season pork shoulder with 1 sachet of Maggi Magic Sarap. Sauté pork in the same pan. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté garlic, onion and bell peppers. Pour tomato sauce and water. Add pork, bay leaf, pepper and sugar. Simmer for 45 minutes.
  4. Add potato and carrot. Season with remaining sachet of Maggi Magic Sarap and simmer for another 10 minutes. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
